Rules to keep in mind

1. Make sure your system is having Mozilla Firefox/ Google Chrome in case of web.

2. Once the exam paper is open for the learners, the exam time starts and logs out on completion of duration of the examination.

3. In case of any malpractice, that particular exam will be locked for concerned learner.

4. To ensure that the registered user is giving the test, the system will be taking snapshots at random time instants so you are supposed to be in front of the camera all the time failing in which you might be debarred from the interview.

5. In case of any technical failure, inform the concerned invigilator immediately.

6. There is +3 marks for each correct answer and -1 for each wrong answer. There is no negative marking for unanswered questions.

7. Mark your answer by checking the appropriate radio button / check box

8. Users are prohibited from using books, notes, slips, reference material, log tables, scribbling pads, cell phones, programmable calculators, electronic and audio/video devices, etc.

9. In case of genuine reason, time will be extended for particular learners if learner registration IDs and system IPs are informed.

10. Browser will be locked for the duration of the exam and System IP will be under continuous observation/monitoring during the examination in order to avoid any kind of malpractice.

11. Any of the actions mentioned below come under malpractice and liable for disciplinary action and they shall not be permitted to continue the examination:

12. To change your answer for single answer questions, please check the radio button that you think is appropriate. The previous answer automatically gets updated with the new answer.

13. A timer for exam is displayed at the top left corner of the page that shows remaining time for your exam.

14. You cannot go back to the exam once you have clicked the Complete Exam Button and confirmed the same.

15. After the expiry of the time limit, the examination will be automatically terminated.

16. You can mark a question for review if you are unsure about the answer.

17. You can browse between questions using the grid navigator. The Answered questions appear as green, the marked for review questions as yellow and the unanswered as white.
